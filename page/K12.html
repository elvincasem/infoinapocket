Scholarships for Graduate Studies

MASTER/DOCTORATE

HEI employee can go on full-time study to earn Master's or Doctorate degrees, with grants that include tuition cost, stipend, book allowance, transportation allowance, and thesis grant (if applicable).

THESIS/DISSERTATION

Faculty with completed master's or doctoral coursework may receive a grant to undertake research for the completion of the corresponding master's thesis or doctoral dissertation.
