SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL SUPPORT GRANTS

SOLO GRANTS

ACTION RESEARCH GRANTS FOR SOLO HEI FACULTY are for teachers who are given teaching loads in SHS during the K to 12 transition period (SY 2016-17 to SY 2020-21). they will propose and implement teaching and learning practices that are evidence-based and well-documented in the form of action research.

UNIT GRANTS

CONTENT KNOWLEDGE DEVELOPMENT GRANTS FOR HEI UNITS are for small groups of HEI faculty members who represent a discipline or a program. They will propose and implement SHS Teacher Development Programs which aim to train and/ or mentor SHS teachers based on content knowledge.
