Professional Advancement Grants

PROFESSIONAL ADVANCEMENT

Qualified faculty and staff may build expertise in their fields of specialization by undertaking non-degree professional advancement courses:

1. Graduate Certificate/Diploma Programs

2. Leadership Development Programs

3. Accreditation of Learning earned through Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs)

Programs taken must be in areas related to the grantee's teaching specialization, research, creative, or extension practice, and that help build local, regional or national competence, consciousness and conscience.

POSTDOCTORAL STUDIES

Faculty and staff with doctoral degrees may undertake research leading to a postdoctoral qualification, hosted by a reputable institution in the Philippines or avroad. Postdoctoral Study Grants are awarded for a maximum duration of one (1) year.
